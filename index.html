intermittent fasting 16:8

On the 16:8 diet, you spend 16 hours a day consuming only unsweetened drinks such as water, tea and coffee. The remaining 8-hour time window is when you consume all your meals and snacks for the day. Many people choose to do so by starting their fast in the evening, skipping breakfast and eating their first meal of the day around lunch time. One of the advantages of this way of eating is that no food is prohibited.
